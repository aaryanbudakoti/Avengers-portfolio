## 🧠 What Even Is This?

Genius-level question. Glad you asked.

This is a **cinematic fan tribute** to Earth's Mightiest Heroes, hand-crafted with the same precision I used to build the Mark I — except with better lighting and no cave involved. It's got heroes, villains, a gallery of our greatest hits, and a recruitment form for people who think they've got what it takes.

Spoiler: they don't. But we appreciate the enthusiasm.
